    What is "Visit Baltics"
    Visit Baltics is a project about interesting places and activities in Latvia, Lithuania and Estonia. This is a project about love for the Baltics. We talk about natural sights and architecture, cafes and restaurants, museums and hotels.
    We create routes
    At our disposal, we have a bunch of ready-made guides to various objects in the Baltic states planned for one-day visits or several-week trips.
    Just choose an interesting route and go discover Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania.
